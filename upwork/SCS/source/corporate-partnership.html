Corporate Partnership

Get national, regional or local exposure for your company that reaches youth, athletes and professionals — and their supporters. Help make sport what it should be. Become a Partner today. The examples below are customizable and flexible please contact us if interested.

  • Gold Partner – SCS Title Sponsor – 250K and above

    1. Co-branding on SCS website header
    2. Logo included on all SCS t-shirts for the year
    3. Logo included on custom-designed SCS t-shirts
    4. Lead marketing position/co-branding at all events
    5. Included in SCS National media and social marketing campaign
    6. Logo linked to partner’s PR marketing content on SCS website
    7. Use of SCS logo allowed on website

  • Silver Partner – National Event Sponsor – 100K and above

    1. Logo included on custom designed SCS t-shirts for partner
    2. Lead marketing position/co-branding at all events
    3. Included in SCS National media and social marketing campaign
    4. Logo linked to partner’s PR marketing content on SCS website
    5. Use of SCS logo allowed on website

  • Bronze Partner- Regional Participation – 25K to 99K

    1. Lead marketing position/co-branding at regional events
    2. Included in SCS Regional Media campaign and social marketing
    3. Logo linked to partner’s PR marketing content on SCS website
    4. Booth space at regional events
    5. Use of SCS logo allowed on website

  • SCS Event Sponsor – Local Participation – 5K to 25K

    1. Lead marketing position/co-branding at local events
    2. Included in SCS Event marketing campaign and social marketing
    3. Logo on materials related to local events on SCS website
    4. Booth space at local events
    5. Use of SCS logo allowed on website

  • SCS Event Co-sponsor – Local Participation – 1K to 5K

    1. Co-branding at local events
    2. Included in SCS Event marketing campaign and social marketing
    3. Logo on materials related to local events on SCS website
    4. Booth space at local events
    5. Use of SCS logo allowed on website
